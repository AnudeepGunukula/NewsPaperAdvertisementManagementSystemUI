Our History

With a spectacular success record and being the finest in advertising, Bookmyad offers excellent classified booking service for the online platform. Employing ingenious and insightful ways for bringing about customer satisfaction, the advertising agency has been instrumental in revamping the whole ad booking system by taking it online.

The agency has carved a niche for itself with regard to Newspaper ads of SME and individual advertisers by giving a free hand to the customers to decide how they want to design their ads. Due to our excellent co-operation, advertisers are further assured of booking their ads in premier newspapers of the country including Hindustan Times, Dainik Jagran, Deccan Herald, Times of India, Punjab Kesari amongst others.
